## Hazard Rate $\lambda$
$1-CPD$ link to S, survial function:$$\lambda(t)=-\frac{\frac{dS}{dt}}{S}$$ thus it is also equal to $$\lambda(t)=\frac{\left( \frac{dCPD}{dt} \right)}{1-CPD}$$
Do the integral, you can get: $S=\exp^{-\int^t_{0}\lambda(x) \, dx}$
